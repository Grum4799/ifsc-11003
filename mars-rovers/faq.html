About Mars

How far is Mars from Earth?

Mars varies from about 54.6 million km to over 400 million km from Earth. Missions take about 6–9 months.

What is the Martian atmosphere like?

Mars has a thin CO₂ atmosphere (~1% of Earth’s pressure), causing extreme temperatures and high radiation.

Did Mars have water?

Yes. Evidence includes river valleys, lake beds, and minerals that form in water.

The Rovers

How do rovers communicate?

They send signals via orbiters or NASA’s Deep Space Network with delays of 3–22 minutes.

Can rovers be driven in real time?

No. Commands are pre-programmed due to signal delay.

Future

Have we found life on Mars?

No confirmed life, but Mars shows strong evidence it was once habitable.

When will humans go to Mars?

Possibly in the 2030s–2040s depending on technology and funding.
